Nigerian Street Food Guide – Website Plan

Site Name

Nigerian Street Food Guide

This name was chosen because the website focuses on introducing visitors to popular Nigerian street foods and where they can be found. The name clearly describes the content of the site and makes it easy for users to understand what the website is about.

Optional Domain: nigerianstreetfoodguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about popular Nigerian street foods such as suya, puff-puff, boli, akara, and roasted corn. The website will explain what each food is, where it is commonly sold, and why it is important in Nigerian culture. The site will also recommend popular places to find street food and provide images of each food.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular street foods in Nigeria?
  • Where can I find the best places to buy street food in Lagos?
  • What ingredients are used to make Nigerian street food like suya or puff-puff?

Color Schema

The site will use the following colors:

Primary Color – Orange (#d35400)

Used for headings, navigation highlights, and accents.

Secondary Color – Dark Blue (#2c3e50)

Used for text, footer background, and navigation bar.

Typography

Heading Font: Arial or similar sans-serif font.

Used for page titles and headings.

Body Font: Helvetica / Arial

Used for paragraphs and content text.
